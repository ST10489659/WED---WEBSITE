Our Services & Programmes

Providing shelter, meals, skills training, and hope for those in need across Cape Town.

What We Offer

Safe Shelters

We provide secure accommodation for individuals experiencing homelessness, giving them a safe place to rest and recover.

Nutritious Meals

Daily nutritious meals are served to ensure our guests stay healthy and regain strength during their stay.

Skills Training

Workshops and training programmes empower our guests with employable skills for reintegration into society.

Reintegration Support

We help reconnect individuals with families, communities, and job opportunities to rebuild their lives.
