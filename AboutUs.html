About Us

Our mission is to help homeless people reintegrate into society with dignity.

Our History

The Haven Night Shelter was founded in 1978 in Cape Town, South Africa, with the aim of providing temporary shelter and rehabilitation services to homeless adults. Today, we operate multiple shelters across the Western Cape, offering not only a bed and a meal, but also access to social services, counselling, and skills development.

Our Mission

To help homeless people reintegrate into society by providing shelter, support, and opportunities.

Our Vision

A South Africa where no one is forced to live on the streets, and every person has the dignity of a safe place to call home.
