Success Stories

We are thrilled to share an inspiring success story that embodies the resilience and determination of the individuals we serve at our homeless shelter. The journey of Mr. and Mrs. S reflects the transformative impact our shelter can have on the lives of those seeking a fresh start. On June 2, 2023, our shelter welcomed a husband and wife who had travelled all the way from the North West Province in South Africa. They sought refuge and a chance to improve their living situation while actively pursuing job opportunities. The wife, facing the additional challenge of a limb disability, was receiving a grant for support. Throughout their stay with us, Mr. and Mrs. S displayed unwavering cooperation and determination. The husband eagerly sought job opportunities, recognizing the importance of financial stability for their future. In November 2023, their perseverance bore fruit when the husband secured a position as a security guard within the community. This employment not only provided them with a source of income but also instilled a renewed sense of purpose and self-sufficiency. By the end of December, buoyed by the husband's employment success, Mr. and Mrs. S were able to put down a deposit on a flat. This achievement marked a significant milestone on their journey to independence. In the spirit of new beginnings, they moved into their own residential place at the beginning of January 2024. The success of Mr. and Mrs. S exemplifies the collaborative efforts of our shelter team in providing support, guidance, and a safe haven for individuals facing adversity. Their journey serves as a testament to the positive impact we can have on the lives of those seeking to overcome homelessness and build a better future.
- Mr. and Mrs. S, Former Residents
